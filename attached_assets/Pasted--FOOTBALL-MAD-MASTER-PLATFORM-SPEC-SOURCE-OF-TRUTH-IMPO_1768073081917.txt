1. Vision

Football Mad will be THE go-to destination for all football fans worldwide.

It combines:

News & analysis

Match, team & player hubs

Transfers & injuries (with trust indicators)

Fantasy Premier League content

Games & predictions (future phases)

Community & fan content

Podcasts & video

Shop & merchandise

Memberships & newsletters

Football Mad is a daily habit, not a blog.

2. Core Product Principles (Non-Negotiable)

Habit over hits – optimise for daily return visits

Personalisation is the product – followed teams drive the experience

Hubs are product pages, not posts

Trust beats speed – transfers & injuries show sources + confidence

Future-proof architecture – leagues, seasons, tools added without rewrites

Everything links together – articles connect to teams, matches, players

Every page has a next action (Follow, Subscribe, Comment, Buy)

3. Tech Stack (Phase 1)

Next.js (App Router)

TypeScript

TailwindCSS

Postgres

Prisma ORM

Auth: NextAuth (credentials + email)

Payments: Stripe Checkout + Webhooks

Search: Postgres full-text (Algolia later)

Hosting: Replit-compatible, monolithic Phase 1
